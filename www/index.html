Quinncia

Quinncia is a job marketplace for students and employers. Our automated interviews, similar to a Skype interview with Siri, help student's highlight their personality and abilities. Hiring managers are interviewed to give insights to job expectations and company culture.

In addition, we partner with university career centers to provide video analysis and feedback tools so advisors can send quality feedback directly to their students.
